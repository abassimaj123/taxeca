App: Canada Sales Tax Calculator — TaxeCA
Languages: English, French

Short Description:
Sales tax calculator for all Canadian provinces — GST/HST/PST/TVQ 2026.

Full Description:
TaxeCA is the fastest and most accurate Canadian sales tax calculator — covering every province and territory with up-to-date 2026 rates.

Whether you are shopping, dining out, or splitting a restaurant bill, TaxeCA gives you the exact breakdown in seconds.

Features:
- GST/HST/PST calculation for all 13 provinces and territories
- Quebec TPS/TVQ exact rates (5% + 9.975%)
- Tip mode: calculate gratuity as a percentage or fixed amount
- Restaurant mode: tax + tip + bill splitting per person
- Reverse calculation: find the pre-tax amount from a total paid
- Shopping list mode: tax multiple items at once
- Calculation history with export to PDF
- Full bilingual support: English and French
- 2026 rates always up to date

Supported provinces and territories:
AB · BC · MB · NB · NL · NS · NT · NU · ON · PE · QC · SK · YT
