App: Canada Tax Calculator — TaxeCA
Languages: English, French

Short Description:
Calculate your Canadian federal and provincial income tax instantly — all provinces, 2024 brackets.

Full Description:
TaxeCA helps individuals estimate their federal and provincial income taxes in Canada quickly and accurately.

Features:
- Federal income tax calculation (2024 brackets)
- Provincial tax for all provinces and territories
- CPP and EI contributions
- Effective tax rate and marginal rate display
- Tax bracket breakdown
- Net income estimator after all taxes
